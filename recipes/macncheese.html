Mac N' Cheese

Description

This creamy, southern style macaoroni and cheese is a favorite at the Dkatt residence! It's a simple recipe that packs a surprising amount of flavor, so much so that your family will be demanding it for dinner night after night!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup butter (unsalted)
  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 gallon milk
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne
  • 1 TBSP salt
  • 1/2 cup blue cheese crumbles
  • 1 lb shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 lb soft American cheese(like Velveeta)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Grease a large baking dish (set this aside for now)
  3. Cook the elbow macaroni in a pot of salted water
  4. Pour cooked macaroni into the baking dish from Step 2
  5. In a heavy saucepan, heat the butter over medium heat
  6. Add flour to butter and stir until very lightly browned
  7. Heat the milk in a DIFFERENT heavy bottom pan over medium heat, whisk in flour+butter mixture and bring to a boil to thicken the sauce
  8. Add salt, cayenne, and cheeses and remove mixture from heat
  9. Stir well until all cheese is dissolved. Pour over the cooked maraconi and mix well
  10. Put it in the oven for 10-15 minutes and serve
