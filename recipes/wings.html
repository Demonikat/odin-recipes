Wings

Description

These aren't your granddad's "hot" wings, these babies are a whole different beast! With a dash of various peppers, these wings aren't for the faint of heart. But with that heat comes a torrent of flavor that will leave your house the go-to location for parties going forward!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Flour
  • Seasonings(Cajun works best!)
  • Egg
  • Buttermilk/Milk
  • Vegetable oil
  • Butter
  • Hot sauce (As hot as you can stand it!)
  • Vinegar
  • Worcestershire sauce (for extra flavor!)
  • Blue cheese or ranch dressing for serving

Directions

  1. Stir together flour, salt, pepper and seasonings
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the egg and buttermilk until they are combined
  3. Dip each piece of chicken into the egg, then the flour, letting rest for 10 minutes
  4. Heat several inches of vegeatble oil to 350 F in a pot. DO NOT BOIL
  5. Fry the boneless wings for 5-6 minutes, or until internal temperature reaches 165 F
  6. Remove the wings and place onto a paper-towel lined plate to drain oil
  7. Heat the butter, hot sauce, vinegar, and Worcestershire sauce in a sauce pan until the butter melts
  8. Whisk vigorously until the sauce thickens up, then add it to a small bowl
  9. Coat the boneless wings with sauce and serve with blue cheese or ranch dressing on the side
